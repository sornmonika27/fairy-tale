Snow White

Snow White is a classic fairy tale about a young princess who is forced to flee into the forest to escape her jealous stepmother, the wicked queen. There, she finds refuge with seven dwarfs who take her in. But the queen's envy knows no bounds, and she disguises herself to poison Snow White with a poisoned apple. Only true love's kiss can awaken Snow White from her death-like slumber, leading to a happily ever after.

By: The Brothers Grimm

Age 4-6

Little Red Riding Hood

Little Red Riding Hood is a cautionary tale about the dangers of talking to strangers. A young girl named Little Red Riding Hood is sent by her mother to visit her grandmother and is warned not to stray from the path. However, she encounters a cunning wolf who tricks her into revealing her destination. The wolf races ahead to the grandmother's house, where he eats her and disguises himself in her clothes, waiting to devour Little Red Riding Hood as well. However, a passing woodsman hears her cries for help and rescues her before it's too late.

By: The Brothers Grimm

Cinderella

Cinderella is a classic fairy tale about a young girl who, despite facing adversity and cruelty from her stepfamily, maintains her kindness and optimism. With the help of her fairy godmother, she attends the royal ball and captures the heart of the prince. But when the clock strikes midnight, she must flee, leaving behind a glass slipper as the only clue to her identity..

By: Charles Perrault

Puss in Boots

Puss in Boots is a fairy tale about a clever cat who uses trickery and cunning to help his master, a poor miller's son, rise to wealth and nobility. Through a series of schemes and adventures, Puss in Boots outsmarts ogres, secures a castle, and wins the hand of a princess for his master.

By:Charles Perrault

Age 7-12

Hansel and Gretel

Hansel and Gretel is a dark fairy tale about two siblings abandoned in the forest by their parents. They stumble upon a house made of candy, owned by a wicked witch who intends to eat them. Through cleverness and bravery, Hansel and Gretel outsmart the witch and escape to return home with treasures from the witch's house..

By: The Brothers Grimm

Jack and the Beanstalk

Jack and the Beanstalk is a classic English fairy tale about a boy named Jack who trades his family's cow for magic beans. The beans grow into a gigantic beanstalk that leads Jack to a land high above the clouds, where he encounters a fearsome giant. With quick thinking and courage, Jack outwits the giant and retrieves treasures to help his family.

By: Unknown
